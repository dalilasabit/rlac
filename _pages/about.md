# About Me
Hi! I’m Dalila, originally from Kazakhstan and currently an undergraduate economics student at NYU Abu Dhabi. I’m interested in understanding how policies and markets shape the world around us and enjoy exploring real-world problems through an economic lens.

Outside of academics, I love photography, dancing, and watching TV series. Creativity is an important part of how I approach both my studies and personal projects.

This website currently serves as my course site for the _Reading Like a Computer_ class I’m taking this semester, but I hope to use it in the future to share other projects, ideas, and work.
